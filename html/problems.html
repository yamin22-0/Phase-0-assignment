The Global Water Crisis

Around the world, countless families face daily water struggles. Pollution, scarcity, and poor sanitation continue to limit access to safe drinking water. Understanding these issues helps us build better solutions.

Key Water Challenges

These are the primary issues affecting communities worldwide

Water Scarcity

Climate change and drought cause many regions to run dry, forcing families to walk long distances for water.

2.3B

People affected

Water Pollution

Chemicals, waste, and plastics contaminate rivers and streams daily, making water unsafe for consumption.

80%

Of wastewater untreated

Water-Borne Diseases

Unsafe water leads to cholera, typhoid, diarrhea and other deadly diseases, especially affecting children.

485K

Annual deaths from diarrhea

Poor Sanitation

Lack of toilets and drainage systems causes waste and water to mix, spreading contamination.

3.6B

Lack safe sanitation
